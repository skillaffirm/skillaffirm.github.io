About SkillAffirm

Originally the Health Check application was part of a bigger platform, in the process we separated it to be a stand alone app due to production costs. The main purpose of the application then became to draw the target audience to the main “paid” Saas portal. So basically it became a freebie that on a certain level prompts the customer to register on the main Saas while giving moderate value. The App was designed to give Ruby developers and dev team managers the ability to check their code for multiple vulnerabilities and while providing some low level insights the app prompted them to sign up and get solution options.
